About the Conference

HDS offers the premiere housing software solution for tribal housing authorities. Our goal is to provide streamlined technology and customer support that makes our clients’ lives easier.

Our conference is the perfect vehicle to develop the skills of current HDS clients, obtain in-depth product trials for prospective clients, and even a beneficial educational tool for those who are not currently HDS clients, but whom want to bring greater understanding and potential software solutions back to their organizations.

By attending the HDS Tribal Housing Conference, you’ll be among the first to get an overview of our brand new, comprehensive software solution for tribal housing authorities: Doorways. You’ll even have a chance to discuss our vision for future capabilities. Dive deeper into various software solutions, and walk away as a Super User!
